About Khazanchi

In the year 1971, Mr. Tarachand Mehta (Jain), a visionary entrepreneur, graced the bustling city of Chennai with his presence, brimming with the ambition to craft magnificent jewellery that would sing to the soul of every woman. And thus, was born the legacy of Khazanchi Jewellers.
