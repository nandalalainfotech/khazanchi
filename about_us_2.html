WHO WE ARE?

Khazanchi Jewellers Limited (Formerly Known as Khazanchi Jewellers Private Limited) The CIN of the Company is U36911TN1996PLC034918. Our registered office and business of operation is based in Chennai situated at No. 130, NSC Bose road, Sowcarpet, Chennai – 600079. Mr. Tarachand Mehta (Jain), Managing Director, a visionary entrepreneur, graced the bustling city of Chennai in the year 1971 with his presence, brimming with the ambition to craft magnificent jewellery. He has started the jewellery business in the corporate office of the company situated at No. 52, NSC Bose road, Sowcarpet, Chennai – 600 079 on his own and later on expanded his business and started the new showroom now the registered office of the company. The company has expanded and grown its business by selling of gold in retail and wholesale sector. Our Company was originally incorporated as Private Limited Company under the Companies Act, 1956 on March 25th, 1996. Subsequently,

Our Company was converted into a public company and consequently name was changed to "Khazanchi Jewellers Limited" (KJL) vide fresh certificate of incorporation dated April 27, 2023 issued by Registrar of Companies, Chennai.


Khazanchi Jewellers Limited has a combination of several key business strategies including

Focus on product quality

Khazanchi Pvt. Ltd. focuses heavily on product quality, using high-quality materials sourced from ethical and sustainable sources. This helps the company differentiate itself from competitors and build a loyal customer base that values quality over quantity.

Competitive pricing

Khazanchi Pvt. Ltd. prices its products competitively, making them accessible to a broad range of customers. This allows the company to appeal to a wider audience and generate repeat business through positive word-of-mouth recommendations.

Customer-centric approach

Khazanchi Pvt. Ltd. prioritizes customer satisfaction, offering personalized service and creating a welcoming shopping environment. By focusing on the needs and preferences of our customers, we have built build strong relationships and a loyal following.

Product Diversification

Khazanchi Pvt. Ltd. provides accessibility to a wide range of products under one single roof. This attracts a wide demographic of clientele. Focusing on fast selling and high demand products ensures a regular and continual footfall.

Focus Strategy

Although Khazanchi Pvt. Ltd. has a diverse portfolio of products it also maintains a strong focus on the traditions and trends prevailing in the market. This is achieved by inventory planning and strategic working capital management ensuring no market opportunity in lost.

Product Diversification

Overall, the business strategy followed by Khazanchi Pvt. Ltd. appears to prioritize quality, affordability, customer satisfaction, diversification, and sustainability, allowing the company to organically build a loyal customer base and generate positive word-of-mouth recommendations along with modern marketing campaigns.
