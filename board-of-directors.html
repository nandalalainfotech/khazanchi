GOUTHAM

Mr. Goutham, our esteemed Executive Director, aged 46 years possesses a wealth of knowledge and expertise garnered over an illustrious career spanning three decades in the captivating world of jewellery. His remarkable journey with our company commenced in 1996 when he assumed the role of Director, and since then, he has been an instrumental figure in propelling our brand to new heights. Throughout his tenure, he has been a driving force behind our company's exponential growth, particularly in expanding our retail and wholesale footprint. With a keen eye for market trends and an unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction, he strategically introduced a vast array of jewellery collections that have captivated a broader spectrum of customers. By diversifying our product offerings, he has ensured that our brand resonates with individuals from all walks of life, fostering inclusivity and embracing the diverse tastes and preferences of our esteemed clientele. He offers strategic guidance, inspires creativity, and empowers our employees to reach their full potential, fostering a unified and harmonious workforce that drives our company's success.

VIKAS MEHTA

Mr. Vikas Mehta, our esteemed Chief Financial Officer aged 41 years who brings a wealth of experience and expertise to our company. At the age of 41, he has been appointed as the CFO of the company starting from May 4, 2023. With a strong educational background, he holds a Commerce degree from the University of Madras. With over 20 years of experience in the field of jewellery, he has honed his skills and developed a deep understanding of the industry. His expertise in finance is particularly noteworthy, and he possesses a comprehensive knowledge of financial management and analysis. As the Chief Financial Officer, he plays a crucial role in overseeing the financial operations of our company. His strategic financial planning and decision-making skills are instrumental in driving our business forward and ensuring its financial stability and growth. His understanding of the specific challenges and opportunities within the industry enables him to navigate complex financial landscapes effectively.
